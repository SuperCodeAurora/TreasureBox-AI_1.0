**A hyper-lightweight, 3-tier multi-agent Citadel that runs a 28-node AI enterprise in your terminal. Zero bloat. Pure logic.**

Treasure Box AI is a highly modular Multi-Agent System (MAS). It abandons the bloated, monolithic designs of traditional AI frameworks. Instead, it uses a **3-Tier Enterprise Architecture** to break down massive tasks, managed by an orchestrator, executed by department heads, and built by junior sub-agents.

## ðŸ›ï¸ The 3-Tier Enterprise Architecture



The Citadel operates on a strict corporate hierarchy to prevent AI hallucinations and context-window collapse. 

1. **Tier 1 (The Orchestrator):** Routes the user's master prompt to the correct departments.
2. **Tier 2 (Department Heads):** Middle management. They break tasks down and manage the laborers. **The Firing Protocol:** Heads have absolute authority to dynamically "fire" (bypass) underperforming helpers during a runtime and complete the task themselves to ensure the project doesn't fail.
3. **Tier 3 (Junior Helpers):** Narrowly scoped sub-agents who do the manual labor.
